About RideBook

Our Mission

RideBook is an open-source initiative designed to revolutionize the bicycle rental industry. Our mission is to create a user-friendly, efficient, and customizable platform that empowers businesses and individuals to set up and manage their own bicycle rental systems.

We believe in the power of sustainable transportation and community-driven development. By making our codebase open and accessible, we aim to foster innovation and collaboration in the cycling community.
